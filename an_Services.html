About Sensor Rich

Do you or someone you know have trouble sitting still? Are they always doing something with their hands or seem "bored" within a few minutes of completing a task? Is someone you know on the autism spectrum (have sensory issues with light, sound, textures, etc.), maybe just sensetive to social situations or been classified as "awkward" by others?

You should place our fidget poppers into these busy hands and see what the hype is all about! With this latest fidget "rage", we have sourced the highest quality producers of this silicone tool. They are designed to combat anxiety, boredom, stress and so many other issues that can lead to trouble in school, work or other social settings. The satisfying "pops" on each side keep kids of all ages engrossed and entertained for longer than most other noisy, aggravating, and non-engaging products.

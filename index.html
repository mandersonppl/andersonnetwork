What is The Anderson Network?

We are a socially responsible parent company, designed to either operate a business solely or alongside a business' creator(s) to maximize profits and enrich others' lives.

The Anderson Network is run by Matt & Tiffany Anderson, a husband and wife duo, who currently reside in Kentucky, just south of Louisville. They are part of a network of like-minded individuals who are seeking financial independence and freedom from a nine-to-five.

Tiffany started her career at a large printing company and then for a large pizza chain as a member of their Accounting department, then on to the Marketing department. She has
over 10 years of experience in Marketing and Accounting, and is the Owner & CEO of
The Anderson Network. She is currently running Sensor Rich, which is a company under
The Anderson Network umbrella. Now that she is finally free of a nine-to-five, she is able
to pursue the business full-time.

Previously, Matt served in the U.S. Army as an Infantryman from 2002 to 2006. After the
Army, he had a number of jobs ranging from assistant foreman of a machine shop to pest
control sales, and is currently a Profit Protection Auditor (past 10 years) for a large electronics
retailer. Currently, he is attending Capella University to pusue a Bachelors in Information
Technology. He would like to move in a website/software development direction to further
assist the company.

The Anderson Network is an idea that has been in the back of our minds for many years.
There have been numerous attempts at different business models throughout our adult lives,
but all of our past stumbles, we feel, have been leading us up to this point.
